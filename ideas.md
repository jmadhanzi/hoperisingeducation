## Idea A — "Warm Authority" (Selected)

**Design Movement:** Modern Corporate Nonprofit with Soft Minimalism

**Core Principles:**
1. Warmth-first hierarchy: orange energy draws the eye, navy grounds it
2. Editorial whitespace — generous breathing room between sections
3. Photography-led storytelling: large, full-bleed imagery with dark overlays
4. Tonal layering instead of heavy borders for depth

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary Orange `#EE701E` — "Rising Hope" — used for CTAs, progress bars, highlights
- Navy `#0D215C` — professional grounding, headers, footers, dark sections
- Green `#4BAF4F` — success, growth, environmental themes
- Background `#F8F9FA` — light, airy, approachable surface
- Emotional intent: optimism + trustworthiness + credibility

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Mobile-first fluid grid (4-col mobile, 12-col desktop)
- Asymmetric hero with text left-aligned on desktop, centered on mobile
- Bento-style project cards grid
- Full-bleed dark sections (navy) alternating with light sections
- Sticky top nav with transparent-to-solid scroll behavior

**Signature Elements:**
1. Orange accent underline dividers beneath section headings
2. Dark navy "impact" sections with orange glow blobs
3. Card images with 16px rounded corners and hover zoom

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Active-scale-95 on all buttons (physical press feel)
- Smooth 200ms transitions on nav, cards, CTAs
- Scroll-triggered fade-in for stats and cards
- Progress bars animate on scroll into view

**Animation:**
- Entrance: fade-up (translateY 20px → 0, opacity 0 → 1, 400ms ease-out)
- Stagger: 80ms between sibling cards
- Button press: scale(0.97) 160ms ease-out
- Nav: background opacity 0 → 1 on scroll past 60px
- Stats counter: count-up animation on scroll into view

**Typography System:**
- Headlines: Manrope 700–800, tight letter-spacing (-0.02em for display)
- Body: Hanken Grotesk 400–500, generous line-height (1.6–1.75)
- Labels/CTAs: Hanken Grotesk 600, uppercase tracking-wider
- Scale: Display 48–64px desktop / 32px mobile, H1 36px, H2 28px, Body 16–18px
